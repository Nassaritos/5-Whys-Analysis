5 Whys Root Cause Analysis

Why #1: Decline in membership engagement and financial instability in certain clusters/entities.
Why #2: Entities in clusters 3 and 4, such as Paraguay and Nicaragua, show reduced participation.
Why #3: Limited tailored operational support and education targeted at smaller entities.
Why #4: Budget allocation favors larger clusters, leaving smaller entities underfunded.
Why #5: Inadequate data centralization and tracking systems for smaller entities.
